John Donahoe

John Donahoe serves as the CEO / President of Nike.

CEO / President

John Joseph Donahoe II (born April 30, 1960)[1] is an American businessman who is the CEO of Nike. Early in his career he worked for Bain & Company, becoming the firm's president and CEO in 1999.[2] He is on the board of directors at Nike,[3] The Bridgespan Group [4] and is chairman of PayPal. Donahoe was named president and CEO of ServiceNow, a cloud company, in February 2017.

Matthew Friend

Matthew Friend, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer

EVP & CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER

Matt Friend serves as EVP & Chief Financial Officer at NIKE, Inc. He joined Nike in 2009 working in Corporate Strategy and Development before becoming Chief Financial Officer of Emerging Markets. Matt later served as Chief Financial Officer of Global Categories, Product and Functions and was subsequently appointed Chief Financial Officer of the Nike Brand.

Hilary Krane

Hilary Krane brings global expertise in corporate law, mergers and acquisitions

EVP, CHIEF ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER & GENERAL COUNSEL

As executive vice president, chief administrative officer and general counsel for NIKE, Inc., Hilary Krane brings expertise in the governance and leadership of large organizations, combined with a strong understanding of the complex and dynamic global marketplace. Krane joined NIKE, Inc. from Levi Strauss & Company, where she held various roles including senior vice president, general counsel and corporate affairs.

Heidi O'Neill

President, Consumer and Marketplace at Nike's

PRESIDENT, CONSUMER AND MARKETPLACE

Heidi O'Neill is the President of Consumer and Marketplace at Nike. O'Neill is pushing the brand forward on sustainability, challenging the company to recycle, refurbish, or donate used or defective products and using wind to power its Paris flagship. Since joining Nike in 1998, Heidi has held a variety of leadership roles, including overseeing Nike's retail and digital commerce as President of Nike Direct
